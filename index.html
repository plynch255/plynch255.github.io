Meet Dr. Lynch

Dr. Patricia Lynch graduated from the New Jersey Dental School after receiving her Masters degree from Fairleigh Dickinson University Dental School in 1985, where she studied the antibiotic treatment of periodontal disease. She has been practicing dentistry in Hoboken since 1991 and specializes in veneers and aesthetic cases. Dr. Lynch is certified in Restylane cosmetic procedures, a Certified Brite Smile provider, and was voted the top certified Invisalign provider in 2003.
